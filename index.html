About us

Whether it’s calculating true cost of goods sold or leading large-scale operational changes, most small business owners don’t have the time, resources, or internal expertise to get it done. Big consulting firms are too expensive, and too theoretical. Humble Oak provides practical, affordable support once reserved for large enterprises, without adding stress to already busy teams.

Founded by brothers Jason and Gary Ghag, Humble Oak Solutions is built on a shared passion for helping businesses run smarter, leaner, and stronger. With over 15 years of combined experience in operations, IT systems, project management, and automation, we specialize in transforming challenges into streamlined solutions. Whether optimizing manufacturing workflows or modernizing IT infrastructure, we bring hands-on expertise, strategic insight, and a humble, hard-working approach to every project we take on.
