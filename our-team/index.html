Our Team

Jason Ghag

Operations & Strategy Specialist • Co-Founder

Co-founder of Humble Oak Solutions. Jason’s an accomplished operations leader with over a decade of experience in business operations, strategic project management, and process improvement.

After years of working closely with small and mid-sized businesses across BC, Jason recognized a common challenge: owners were overwhelmed. Whether it was calculating true cost of goods sold or leading large-scale operational changes, most didn’t have the time, resources, or internal expertise to get it done. Big consulting firms were too expensive and too theoretical. Jason founded Humble Oak Solutions to change that.

The firm’s built for hands-on business owners who need results. Together with his brother, Humble Oak delivers practical, affordable support once reserved for large enterprises—without adding pressure to already busy teams.

Jason’s background is in operations strategy and industrial project execution. He has led multimillion-dollar transformations, secured millions in government funding, modernized manufacturing facilities, and streamlined supply chains from end to end.

If you always do what you always did, you’ll always get what you’ve always got.— Henry Ford

Gary Ghag

Business IT Specialist • Co-Founder

Over the years, Gary has built a broad and deep skill set across the IT and business operations landscape, supporting and optimising systems for operations, supply chain, and engineering teams alike.

After years of designing and managing IT systems for growing businesses, Gary noticed a clear pattern: teams were working hard to keep up with modern technology but often didn’t have the right support to do it efficiently.

That gap inspired Gary to co-found Humble Oak Solutions with his brother: a consulting practice built to provide smart, affordable IT and systems expertise tailored to the needs of smaller organizations.

His expertise spans MS365, business intelligence, ERP/PLM support, web development, IT infrastructure, and automation. He has led full-scale system deployments, built dashboards, developed secure modern websites, and designed automations that saved organizations hundreds of hours.

At his core, Gary’s a builder—not just of tools and systems, but of stability, clarity, and confidence. He believes technology should empower, not overwhelm.
